About Us

Established in 1977 & headquartered in Mumbai, Shree Jalaram Group has diverse interests from Online Lottery,
Real Estate, Beverage, Healthcare, Automobiles and Online Games etc. Golden Technology is its focused foray
into developing unique and innovative game content for the Indian and International Markets.

Golden Technology prides itself as a straightforward company with a direct and simple approach towards complex problems.
The business is built on integrity, knowledge and professionalism, enabling partners and stakeholders alike to put their trust.
Golden Technology has appointed excellent human capital. It consists of Gen-Y Techies and offers fantastic opportunity,
open work ecosystem combined with visible career growth opportunities. The company infused momentous effort for right
candidates and a long term vision to create breath-taking game experience.

Globally Technology is a part of an entertainment matrix but has experienced dramatic trend change at regular intervals,like
penetration of smart devices is reflective of mobile games and mobile friendly User Interface (UI). Golden Technology
understands this challenges and expects to deliver under such environments.
